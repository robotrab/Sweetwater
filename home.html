It is our honor to have you visit with us online at Sweetwater Branch Baptist Church. It is my desire as the Pastor to preach and lead God's Church in a direction that leads people to a fervent relationship with Christ. My prayer and purpose as a minister of the Gospel of Jesus Christ is see lost souls saved, broken lives put back together, and people living holy lives before God and man. I would like to cordially invite you and your family to come and worship with us on Sunday in the AM or PM Service and Wednesday PM Service.

Love in Christ,

Pastor Charles C. Mixon
